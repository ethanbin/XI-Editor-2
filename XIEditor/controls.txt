Command Mode:
	Navigation: 
		up:				k
		down:			j
		right:			l
		left:			h

	Delete a character:				x

	Undo:							u

	Enter Insert mode:
		At cursor:					i
		At the start of the line:	I

	Insert newline & enter Insert mode:
		Below the cursor:			o
		Above the cursor:			O

	Exit Insert mode:				ESC

	Enter Last Line mode:			:
	Last Line mode commands:
		quit:						q
		write (save):				w
		write (save) and quit:		wq

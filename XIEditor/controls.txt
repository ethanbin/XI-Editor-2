Command Mode:
	Navigation: 
		up:				k
		down:			j
		right:			l
		left:			h

	Delete a character:				x

	Undo:							u

	Enter insert mode:
		At cursor:					i
		At the start of the line:	I

	Insert newline & enter insert mode:
		Below the cursor:			o
		Above the cursor:			O

	Exit insert mode:				ESC

	Exit xi editor:					:q
